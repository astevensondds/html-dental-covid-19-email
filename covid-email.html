Important information for patients
 


Dear patients,

We hope that this email finds you well and with clean hands. In light of the public health crisis that has made its way into all of our daily lives, we have determined that our part to play for the safety of you, our staff, and the community at large is to pause all non-emergent dental treatment beginning Wednesday, March 18th. This follows the recommendations of the American Dental Association and the Iowa Dental Association, and mirrors steps taken by the University of Iowa College of Dentistry & Dental Clinics. We have begun making every attempt to contact patients with upcoming appointments and will continue to do so over the coming days as the situation hopefully clarifies for us all.

We are developing contingency plans to deal with emergencies as they arise and to make sure that our staff is provided for. We are also working to partner with other local dentists to make sure that our profession is able to do all we can to relieve pressure on local health systems and make sure that personal protective equipment is available for those on the front lines.
